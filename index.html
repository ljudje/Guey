In the lively heart of Mexico's agave-rich lands, an unlikely pair of friends thrived—the easygoing dog and the rooster, an aspiring musician. From their village, they shared countless sunrises, laughter, and dreams. However, as life unfurled, their paths diverged. While the dog stayed to nurture the land of his forefathers, the rooster's adventures took him to the stages of many cities around the world. From Mexico City to Paris & New York, the rooster has seen it all. And the dog, well he committed himself to growing agave and making Mezcal. Years have passed and it was at the village festival when the rooster and the dog stumbled upon each other. Out of surprise they shouted to each '¡Hola, Güey!' Their joyous reunion put them in an embrace and the dog offered the rooster a pour of Mezcal. This Mezcal was made in the same manner as his father made it, and his grandfather before. They traded their stories throughout the night and evoked friendship that was not lost, maybe slightly forgotten but brought back to life with every new sip of Mezcal. Bridging tradition with friendship, through every sip they created new timeless memories.
